Frequently asked questions

Do I need to be technical?

No. This is specifically designed for people who aren't. If you can describe the apps you use and how you use them, that's enough.

What if I can't afford $175?

Sliding scale starts at $40, no questions asked. Email me at zam@zamdeshields.org before booking and we'll figure it out. I'd rather have you in the room at a lower rate than not at all.

What will I actually walk away with?

A plain-language read on where your biggest risks are, three to five concrete things to do, and a written one-page summary emailed within 48 hours of our session.

Is this for businesses or individuals?

Individuals. If you run an organization and want training for your team, that's a different kind of engagement — take a look at my organizational services or get in touch directly.

Can I give this as a gift?

Yes. Email me and I'll send you a gift link you can forward. Some of my favorite sessions have been birthday or holiday gifts to family members who'd been meaning to "get around to" their digital privacy for years.
