Services

What ZD LLC Offers

Engagements range from one-time talks to multi-year partnerships.

Service areas

Strategic Consulting

Technology strategy, data governance frameworks, and AI adoption planning for tribal governments, nonprofits, community organizations, and institutions — including those moving toward open-source infrastructure or working on advocacy-focused technology issues.

Training and Workshops

Half-day and full-day sessions on AI literacy, data protection, and sovereignty-centered technology practice. Tailored for specific communities: tribal governments, nonprofits, disability-adjacent organizations, advocacy groups, and professional communities navigating AI in high-stakes contexts.

Speaking and Keynotes

Keynotes and invited talks on Indigenous data sovereignty, AI risk, and the politics of who controls data. Available for conferences, symposia, and institutional audiences.

Technical Assistance

Hands-on support for organizations building or evaluating local AI infrastructure, data policies, and governance frameworks — including groups moving toward open-source tools and community-controlled systems.

AI Training for Specific Communities

Specialized training for defined professional groups: scientists, clinicians, counselors, educators, community organizers, legal professionals, and others navigating AI adoption in high-stakes contexts. Sessions are tailored to the specific concerns and risk landscape of each community.

Facilitation

Structured facilitation for community processes, strategic planning sessions, and multi-stakeholder conversations where technology and values intersect — including organizations working at the intersection of disability justice, data rights, and digital infrastructure.
